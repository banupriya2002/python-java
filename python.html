    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    What is a Full Stack Developer?

    A Full Stack Developer is someone who is proficient in both frontend and backend technologies, capable of building complete web applications.

    What technologies are taught in this course?

    The course covers HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Python (Django/Flask), and database management (MySQL/PostgreSQL).

    Do I need prior experience in programming?

    No, this course is designed for beginners. You'll start with basic programming concepts and progress to more advanced topics.

    How long will it take to complete the course?

    The course typically takes around 3-6 months to complete, depending on your pace.

    Will I get support during the course?

    Yes, you will receive support through forums, live sessions, and Q&A with instructors.
